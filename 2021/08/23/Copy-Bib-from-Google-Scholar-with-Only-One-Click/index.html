Copy Bib from Google Scholar with Only One Click

Posted on 2021-08-23 Edited on 2023-09-03 Disqus:
Symbols count in article: 509 Reading time ≈ 2 mins.
By Z.H. Fu
https://fuzihaofzh.github.io/blog/

Copying bibliography information from Google Scholar entries typically involves multiple steps and clicks. It’s a process that can be time-consuming and often disrupts the flow of your research. Recognizing this challenge, we present the Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib, a Tampermonkey plugin that simplifies this process down to a single click.

Introducing Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib

Designed by maplewizard, the Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib is a Tampermonkey plugin that automates the process of copying bibliography information from Google Scholar entries. With this plugin, you no longer need to go through the multiple steps of copying and pasting bib information. Simply click the “Copy Bib” button for each entry. The page will momentarily refresh, and the bib information will be stored directly into your clipboard.

Installation Guide

Before you can enjoy the convenience brought by Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib, you need to install the Tampermonkey userscript manager. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Install Tampermonkey

  1. Open your preferred web browser and visit the Tampermonkey website (https://www.tampermonkey.net/).
  2. Click the “Download” button.
  3. Choose the version that suits your web browser.
  4. Follow the instructions provided by the installer to complete the installation.

Step 2: Install Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib

Once Tampermonkey is installed, you can proceed to install the Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib plugin:

  1. Open your web browser and go to the Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib page on Greasyfork (https://greasyfork.org/en/scripts/429933-google-scholar-oneclick-copy-bib).
  2. Click the “Install this script” button.
  3. Tampermonkey will open a new tab displaying the script. Click “Install” to add the script to Tampermonkey.

And there you have it! You’ve successfully installed Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib.

How to Use Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib

Once you have successfully installed the Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib plugin, using it is incredibly straightforward. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Open any Google Scholar page. If the plugin is running correctly, you should see a new button labeled “Copy Bib” for each entry.

  1. To copy the bib information of an entry, simply click the “Copy Bib” button. The page will refresh momentarily, and then the bib information will be automatically stored in your clipboard.

And that’s it! You can now easily paste the bib information wherever you need it, all thanks to Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib. This tool is designed to make your research process more efficient, allowing you to focus more on your work and less on administrative tasks. Start using it today and experience the convenience it brings to your research workflow.

Wrapping Up

Google Scholar Oneclick Copy Bib is a powerful tool that simplifies your research process. By reducing the task of copying bib information to a single click, it lets you concentrate more on your research and less on administrative tasks. Experience the ease and efficiency it brings to your research workflow today.
